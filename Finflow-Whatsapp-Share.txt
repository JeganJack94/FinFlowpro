Finflow: Track Your Finances Easily!

Hey! Check out Finflow, a simple app to track your income, expenses, and savings. It works on your phone and you can even install it like an app!

Try it here: https://fin-flowai.vercel.app/

- Free to use
- Works offline
- Super easy to use

How to install Finflow as an app (PWA):
- On Android (Chrome): Open the link, tap the three-dot menu, select "Add to Home screen," then tap "Add."
- On iPhone (Safari): Open the link, tap the Share icon, select "Add to Home Screen," then tap "Add."
- On Desktop: Open the link, look for the install icon in the address bar or browser menu, and click "Install."
